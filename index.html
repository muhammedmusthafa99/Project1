Karma-colors is an art-outsourcing and development studio founded in 2014 with offices in Vienna and Hong-kong. With a decade of experience in providing a full range of services such as art design (2D and 3D), animation, effect design and sound design, we always strive to provide the highest quality. We do not just offer our services, we offer our expertise and our passion. Our major aim is to provide you with the highest quality services within the stated time-frame and budget constraints. We do not look at projects as a one-time service rather a long term partnership. Our history and portfolio is the best justification for this claim!
